Solar Automation System

A custom operations platform for solar teams that needed faster proposal generation, cleaner field coordination, and better visibility into work happening across sites and workflows.

Client Type

Solar and field operations team

Use Case

Workflow automation, reporting

Scope

Operations and dashboard platform

Stack

Next.js, APIs, automation logic

The Challenge

The operation needed better control over field activity, proposal workflow, and internal coordination. Too much work depended on manual updates, delayed reporting, and fragmented visibility across what had been completed, what was pending, and where teams needed to act next.

Slow Proposal Flow
Proposal generation and operational updates were slower than the business needed to maintain momentum.
Weak Status Visibility
Teams lacked a reliable system for understanding live progress, issues, and work status across the operation.
Manual Coordination Load
Too many handoffs relied on follow-up and repetitive reporting instead of structured workflow tracking.

What We Built

We built a custom operations platform that improved workflow movement, made key activity more visible, and supported faster execution for teams managing field-driven work.

Workflow Automation
Structured process flows to reduce manual coordination and improve handoff clarity.
Live Operational Dashboards
A clearer view of current activity, bottlenecks, and status across key workstreams.
Reporting Layer
Automated summaries and operational visibility to reduce repetitive reporting effort.

Delivered Scope

The system focused on speed, visibility, and operational throughput rather than generic feature bloat.

  • Proposal and process workflow support
  • Dashboard views for managers and operations users
  • Status-driven task visibility across the workflow
  • Reporting output for day-to-day operational review
  • Automation logic to reduce repetitive coordination work

Better visibility into ongoing operations
