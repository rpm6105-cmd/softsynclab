Overview

A growing team, a growing operational gap

Go Solar Solutions operates a mixed workforce — field installation teams, sales staff, and back-office personnel — spread across project sites and a central office in Navi Mumbai. As the team grew, the administrative effort required to manage employees, run monthly payroll, and maintain statutory compliance grew with it.

SoftSync Solutions was engaged to design and build a custom HRMS and Payroll platform tailored to the company's workflows — one that could handle their full employee lifecycle, automate month-end payroll processing, and provide a reliable foundation for compliance record-keeping.

The Challenge

What the team was dealing with

No Centralised Employee Records
Employee data — joining dates, designations, compensation details, document copies — lived in multiple spreadsheets, folders, and email threads. There was no single reliable source of truth for HR information.
Manual Payroll Every Month
Salary calculations involved manual cross-referencing of attendance data, leave records, overtime inputs, and deduction rules. The process was time-consuming and left room for errors that were only caught after payslips had already gone out.
Compliance Tracking Done Reactively
PF, ESI, PT, and TDS records were maintained separately, often updated in response to filing deadlines rather than as part of an integrated workflow. This created last-minute pressure and inconsistencies across filing periods.
Attendance Visibility Was Limited
For field teams especially, tracking daily attendance, site-based absences, and leave requests depended on manager calls and informal check-ins. Nothing was formally logged in one place.
No Self-Service for Employees
Every payslip request, leave application, or document query came to the HR admin directly — adding unnecessary back-and-forth and slowing down routine tasks for both employees and the team managing them.
Scaling Was Becoming Harder
As headcount grew, the limitations of the existing approach became more pronounced. Adding more people meant proportionally more admin effort — the team needed a system that could scale without scaling the manual workload with it.

What SoftSync Built

We designed the system around how Go Solar's HR and operations team actually worked — not around a generic feature checklist. The result is a platform they own, understand, and can operate without external dependency.

Centralised HR Management
A single employee master covering onboarding records, department structure, role history, documents, and employment status — accessible and searchable by HR and management.
Payroll Engine with Deduction Logic
Monthly payroll runs that pull from live attendance and leave data, apply configured deduction and allowance rules, and generate payslips ready for distribution — with an approval step before finalisation.
Compliance Workflow Layer
Structured monthly compliance tracking for PF, ESI, PT, and TDS — with calculation support, record export, and a clear audit trail that makes filing periods less stressful for the team handling them.

Delivered Scope

The build focused on operational reliability and practical daily use — every feature included has a clear purpose in their workflow.

  • Employee master and profile management with document storage
  • Attendance tracking with daily entry, exception handling, and monthly review
  • Leave management — application, approval, carry-forward, and balance tracking
  • Payroll engine with configurable salary structure, allowances, and deductions
  • Automated payslip generation and employee-facing download access
  • PF, ESI, PT, and TDS compliance modules with monthly calculation and export
  • Role-based access — HR admin, manager, and employee views
  • Reporting layer for attendance summaries, payroll registers, and compliance logs
  • Admin dashboard with headcount, payroll cost, and leave status overview

Deployment & Support

From build to live operation

Delivery was structured to give the Go Solar team a stable, tested system from day one — with direct support for onboarding and any post-launch operational queries.

01

Discovery & Scope Definition

Detailed requirement sessions with Go Solar's HR and operations lead to map existing workflows, define data structures, and agree on module scope and phasing.

02

Database Architecture & Core Build

Employee master, attendance, leave, and payroll schema built in PostgreSQL with row-level security policies. Core HR and payroll modules built and internally tested.

03

Compliance Module Integration

PF, ESI, PT, and TDS calculation logic integrated and validated against India-specific statutory requirements. Export formats structured for standard filing workflows.

04

Client Testing & Data Migration

Go Solar's team was given access to a staging environment for testing with real scenarios. Existing employee data was cleaned, structured, and migrated into the new system.

05

Go-Live & Handover

System went live with full production deployment. HR team was trained on day-to-day operations. Handover documentation and admin access provided to the client.
