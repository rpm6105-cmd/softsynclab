The operational problems most solar companies face

If you run a solar EPC or installation company in India, you'll recognise these. This is exactly what Go Solar's team was dealing with before we built their platform.

📂

HR records in multiple places

Employee data spread across spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and email folders — no single reliable record for audit or onboarding.

🕐

Field attendance impossible to track

Technicians on site had no formal attendance logging. Tracking happened over calls — nothing connected to payroll.

💸

Payroll took days every month

Manual data pulling, formula errors, and last-minute corrections. Salary delays damaged team trust.

🏛️

PF/ESI always a scramble

Compliance filings done reactively under deadline pressure. No running record — just panic before due dates.

AMC service falling through gaps

No system for tracking which installed solar sites were due for service. Renewals and visit history managed manually.

📦

No solar equipment visibility

Stock dispatched to sites, no record of what went where. Low-stock situations only noticed when it was already a problem.
