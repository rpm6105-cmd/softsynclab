The Challenge

What a growing solar company was dealing with

Go Solar Solutions operates field installation teams, sales staff, and back-office personnel across Navi Mumbai and project sites. As the team expanded, the manual overhead of managing them became a real operational problem.

📂

No Central Employee Records

Employee data, contracts, documents, and compensation details were spread across spreadsheets and email folders — unreliable and hard to audit.

🕐

Field Attendance Was Informal

Technicians working across multiple sites had no formal attendance system. Tracking was done via calls and messages — impossible to tie cleanly to payroll.

💸

Monthly Payroll Was Manual Assembly

Payroll meant pulling data from multiple sources, calculating deductions by hand, and reviewing everything twice. It took days and still had errors.

🏛️

Compliance Handled Last-Minute

PF, ESI, PT, and TDS records were updated reactively — only when filing deadlines forced it. No structured monthly tracking existed.

🔧

AMC Service Had No System

Annual Maintenance Contracts for installed solar systems had no dedicated tracking. Service schedules, visit history, and renewal alerts all lived in someone's head.

📦

Solar Inventory Was Untracked

Equipment dispatched to sites, returned stock, and low-stock situations had no centralised visibility. Procurement decisions were made on instinct, not data.

The Solution

One platform. Every solar workflow.

We didn't adapt a generic HR tool. We built a platform from scratch that reflects the actual structure of a solar company — with solar site types, solar equipment categories, field-team attendance patterns, and AMC service tracking built into the foundation.

The result is a system that Go Solar's HR, operations, and management teams use every day — because it was designed around how they actually work, not around a product checklist.

Solar-specific role taxonomy — Tech Head, Solar Technician, Solar Design Engineer, Solar Sales Manager and more — built into the employee management module from day one.

Field-aware attendance engine — handles comp-off for technicians who work on holidays, late-arrival slabs with configurable grace periods, and attendance import for bulk entry.

Solar AMC module — tracks every installed site, generates AMC contracts with visit frequency, auto-schedules service visits, and surfaces expiry and maintenance alerts.

Solar inventory control — stock management for 16 solar equipment categories including PV modules, inverters, ACDB, DCDB, earthing kits, cables, and more.

Payroll Engine

Payroll built for solar workforce complexity

Solar companies have payroll complexity that generic tools don't handle well — technicians earning overtime on site visits, incentives for sales closures, field advances, and multiple deduction types running simultaneously.

Configurable late deduction slabs — 20% for 9:45–10:00, 30% for 10:00–10:30, 50% post 10:30 — or full grace. Applied automatically each month.

OT and incentive input — HR can enter per-employee overtime hours and incentive amounts before the payroll run, with a draft save and review step.

Loan and advance auto-recovery — outstanding loan EMIs and advance balances are pulled into payroll deductions automatically from the loans module.

Draft → lock flow — payroll is calculated as a draft, reviewed by HR, then locked before payslips are generated. Locked payrolls can be reopened with a reason.

Full export — payroll register, salary statements, and compliance-ready data exports for PF/ESI/PT/TDS filing.

Compliance

Statutory compliance that keeps up every month

Compliance for a solar company isn't simpler than for any other employer — PF, ESI, PT, and TDS all apply, and falling behind creates real risk. The platform maintains running compliance records month-to-month so filing periods don't become fire-drills.

EPF (PF) — employee and employer contribution calculation per payroll run, with export for monthly ECR filing.

ESIC — ESI applicability check per employee, contribution calculation, and monthly record with export.

Professional Tax — state-wise PT slab calculation (Maharashtra), deducted and tracked per employee monthly.

TDS & Form 16 — TDS tracking against salary and a dedicated Form 16 generation module for annual filing.
